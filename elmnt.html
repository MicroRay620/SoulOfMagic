Magic Systems

Soul of Magic has 3 magic systems, each unique. The three systems are Elemental, Sigil, and Contract systems. While this page won't go in-depth into each system, it will give the generalizations of the 3 systems.

Elemental System

The elemental system is the most common of the 3 systems, only ahead of the Sigil magic system. This system has 3 types of elements, this article will only talk about one of the types. The three types are Extreme, Base, and Combined. This article will only talk about the Base elements, which are the following:

Base Elements

  • Air
  • Earth
  • Fire
  • Water
